Detailed Description

A uiobject that can be rendered in a layer.

Layered regions can display content on the screen (texture, texts, 3D models, ...) and must be contained inside a layer, within a lxgui::gui::frame object. The frame will then render all its layered regions, sorted by layers.

Layered regions cannot themselves react to events; this must be taken care of by the parent frame.
